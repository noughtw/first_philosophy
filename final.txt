been there done that, after all is told and done
"what's then?" is the question

as those projects named first and second philosophy have been so colossal that they keep us so entertained running in circles, we have been distracted from the ever waiting question looming to be un-hidden - the question of final philosophy

one must try to ask for the fun of it literally and genuinely for once, what's after all when the fundamentals are told and done?

for the answer should not be "be boring", nor be "in a pursuit" of a project as conventionally told
we should not appeal to the blessing named "finitude" or "forgetfulness" or "hiddenness" to cope out of the question
we grant us to be as magical as there is to be, and it can be done in so far as it is not incoherent as done at all
"what's then?" is still the question
